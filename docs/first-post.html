    First Post

    Phew, just finished the basic setup of my brand-new blog-site. It took almost five hours to get here. Working with Octopress was fun. Setting up a blog on commandline is way cooler than doing it the wordpress style. Of course, Octopress drew me to itself with its subtitle:

    A blogging framework for hackers.

    I noticed Divya Manian move her blog to Octopress. After looking up Octopress and what people feel about it (everyone seems to like it), I decided to try it myself.
